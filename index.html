FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is FORMA free?

Yes — you can draw for free with the core canvas and a starter set of brushes. FORMA Premium unlocks the full brush library and pro tools via a yearly subscription ($29.99/yr with a 7-day free trial), a monthly plan ($4.99/mo), or a one-time Lifetime purchase ($59.99).

Do I need an Apple Pencil?

No. FORMA works great with your finger, and adds pressure-sensitive size and flow when you draw with an Apple Pencil on a supported iPad.

Where is my artwork stored?

Entirely on your device. FORMA has no account and no cloud — your drawings never leave your iPhone or iPad. You can export to PNG or JPEG and share whenever you like.

What can I export?

You can flatten your canvas to PNG or JPEG, save it to Photos, or send it through the iOS share sheet to any app.

Does FORMA have AI, collaboration or cloud sync?

Not yet. AI tools, live collaboration, cloud sync, timelapse and PSD/SVG export are on our roadmap for future updates — they are not part of the current version.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the App Store → your account → Subscriptions → FORMA → Cancel. You keep Premium until the end of the current period. The Lifetime purchase never expires.

Visit our Support page or email support@forma.app.
