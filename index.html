FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is FORMA free?

Yes — you can draw for free with the core canvas and a starter set of brushes. FORMA Premium unlocks the full brush library and pro tools via a yearly subscription ($29.99/yr with a 7-day free trial), a monthly plan ($4.99/mo), or a one-time Lifetime purchase ($59.99).

Does it work on Mac?

Yes. FORMA is universal — buy it once and use it on iPhone, iPad and Mac, with finger, Apple Pencil, mouse or trackpad.

Do I need an Apple Pencil?

No. FORMA works great with your finger or a mouse/trackpad, and adds pressure-sensitive size and flow when you draw with an Apple Pencil on a supported iPad.

Where is my artwork stored?

Entirely on your device. FORMA has no account and no cloud — your drawings never leave your devices. You can export to PNG or JPEG and share whenever you like.

How does the on-device AI work?

FORMA uses Apple Intelligence to generate reference art from a prompt or your sketch, running on your device — there’s no cloud round-trip and your prompts stay private.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the App Store → your account → Subscriptions → FORMA → Cancel. You keep Premium until the end of the current period. The Lifetime purchase never expires.
