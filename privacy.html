Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026

FORMA ("we", "us", "the app") is built privacy-first. This policy explains what we do — and, importantly, what we don't do — with your data. In short: FORMA collects no personal data.

Your artwork stays on your device

All drawing, layer compositing, editing and storage happen locally on your device. Your canvases, layers, brushes and exported images are stored only on your iPhone or iPad. We do not upload, collect, view or store any of the artwork you create in FORMA. It never reaches us or any third party.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not require an account, email or sign-in.
  • We do not collect personal information.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking of any kind.
  • We do not show ads.
  • We do not collect, sell or share your drawings or any data.

No tracking

FORMA contains no third-party analytics, advertising or tracking SDKs. We do not build a profile of you, and we do not track you across apps or websites.

Purchases

FORMA Premium subscriptions and the Lifetime purchase are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details. Apple handles these transactions and any related data under Apple's Privacy Policy.

Permissions

  • Photos — only used when you choose to save an exported drawing to your photo library, or import an image. FORMA accesses only what you explicitly select.

FORMA requests permissions only at the moment you use a feature that needs one, and only for that purpose.

Children

FORMA is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any material changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email us at support@forma.app.
